# 🌥️ Cloud Native Community Brussels 🇧

> [!TIP]
> We are the official CNCF Community Chapter in Belgium. [Reference](https://github.com/cncf/communitygroups/issues/138)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> While our name is localized to Brussels, we serve the entire Belgian cloud native community.

> Bringing people together for inspiration, collaboration, and fellowship.

We’re a community of enthusiasts, professionals, and newcomers passionate about the Cloud Native ecosystem.  
From Kubernetes to containers, observability to edge—if it's part of the CNCF landscape, we're into it.

Join us on Slack and LinkedIn, or check the community page for upcoming events.

## ✨ Our Mission

To connect people, companies, and technologies in Belgium through fun, inclusive, and hands-on experiences—meetups today, and open to other kinds of events in the future.

We foster an **open, welcoming environment**—regardless of background, age, gender, or race.
